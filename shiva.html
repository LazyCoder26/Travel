Adiyogi Shiva Statue

The Adiyogi Shiva Statue is a 112-foot-tall statue located at the Isha Yoga Center in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva, who is considered the first yogi or Adiyogi in the yogic tradition. The majestic statue stands as a symbol of yoga, meditation, and inner transformation.

The Adiyogi Shiva Statue was consecrated by Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev, a renowned spiritual leader and founder of the Isha Foundation. It was unveiled on Mahashivaratri in 2017 and has since become a significant pilgrimage site for spiritual seekers and yoga enthusiasts from around the world.

The unique architecture of the statue depicts Lord Shiva in his meditative posture, surrounded by the seven chakras, representing the seven dimensions of human consciousness. The serene and tranquil ambiance of the Isha Yoga Center complements the spiritual experience of visitors.
